&nbsp; &nbsp; I'm an undergraduate student in **Computer Science Engineering, Sogang University [@SGCSE](https://cs.sogang.ac.kr/cs/index_new.html)** and an UG Intern of **DISCOS Laboratory [@DISCOS](http://discos.sogang.ac.kr/)**. My research focuses on optimizing key-value stores and distributed file systems on advanced memory & SSD technologies including NVMe SSD, CSD, NVM and CXL, with a specific interest in supporting data-intensive applications such as Clouds, Deep Learning and GPT. I'm also studying system-level optimizations for general applications, based on a deep understanding for underlying structures of operating systems. In summary, I'm getting ready to become a professional on computer systems. No matter what field I go into later, **_my ultimate goal is becoming an awesome system researcher who makes the world covenient_**.
